— Our Story —

Born From Light,
Built to Last.

ORIGIN was founded in 2014 in the sun-drenched hills of Lyon, France, with a singular vision: that clothing should feel as extraordinary as it looks. We believe true luxury lives in the details — the weight of a fabric, the fall of a hem, the integrity of every seam.

Every garment we create begins with the most responsibly sourced materials — organic linens from Belgium, Supima cotton from California, peace silks from India — and is crafted by skilled artisans who share our commitment to excellence.
